Library

“Books are a uniquely portable magic” It is well said that Books give a soul to the universe, Wings to the mind, Flight to the imagination And life to everything. The primary objective of a library is to provide the right information at the right time in the right form to the students. It fosters a learning environment that encourages investigation allows for independent thinking and develops effective study habits for all age groups. It also provides materials that support curriculum, student’s interests and staff needs. We too have a wonderful library set up with high class amenities. The library is supported with E- learning set up, books for the age group ranging from 3 years to 15 years i.e. Nur. to Std X. The library books consists of fiction, novels, adventures etc.
