Computer lab:-

“Computers are magnificent tools for the realization of our dreams”. Computer is an amazing gift of science tools with the new technologies coming up everyday computers have become like windows through which we can watch the world virtually without boundaries. Computer education in school plays a major net worthy role in the career development of young children. The school going children learn new skills and abilities in education. Innovation in computer technology has a profound impact on education. We at SBOA have two equipped computer lab having 50 computers where in the students from std I to std X learn different applications software and computer languages.

INNOVATION TO DIGITAL EDUCATION

First step in this direction was installation of Tata Class edge technology in the year 2017-18 . At present we have Tata Class edge outlet installed in 21 classrooms. This has rendered both Teaching and learning more meaningful and interesting. Its multiple learning experience model is developed on premise that when students use multiple senses and are involved in variety of plan activities. They will be better involved in learning process and will retain the concepts better.

INTIGRATED SCHOOL MANAGEMENT SOFTWARE

Next step in digitization is SBOA Public School started using a web based software named DIVINE CAMPUS for school management in the year 2018-19. It provides the facilities like Attendance of students &teachers, certificates and Documents, fee & finance, HR & Payroll inventory &purchase, transportation management etc. are functioning presently. Migration to the Divine Campus platform has ensured effective control over attendance &fee,circulars, Homework etc. This has also reduce a lot of duplication of the work, where by Teachers can devote more time towards the academic.

COMPETITIVE EXAM

The students proved their flair by achieving spectacular success at Scholarship Exam 26 students of Std V and 37 students of Std VIII qualified this exam & are now eligible for Govt. Scholarship for the 3 consecutive years.

Science lab

“Somewhere, something incredible is waiting to be known” The aim of teaching science is to encourage and enable students to develop inquiring minds and curiosity about science and the natural world. It is an image of the truth. We at SBOA believe that science lab equipments allow students to interact directly with the data gathered. They get first hand learning experience by performing various experiments on their own. School science lab equipments make teaching and learning easy and interesting for the students. The lab experiences enhance mastery of science , develop scientific reasoning abilities, increasing understanding of the complexity and ambiguity of empirical work. We at SBOA have a well equipped lab with specimens, models, slides, chemicals , apparatus etc. for performing various experiments related to physics , chemistry and biology. The students handle these equipments under the supervision of our science teachers and lab Assistant. It helps the students to eradicate superstitious and rigid customs prevailing in so.
