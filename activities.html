ANNUAL FUNCTION

Theme based Annual function in five sessions over the span of 3 days is another special feature of the school. Under the able guidence of our teachers , the students prepare beautyful invitation cards , Programme cards , floral bouquet , gift articals etc… to be presented to the guests. Every deserving candidate gets the platform to exhibit his hidden talent. The school has performed on various themes depicting Indian culture and tradition. This year too the students are going to perform on yet another interesting theme GeetRamayan, Maharashtra Darshan , Folk Dances of India ,

Co-curricular Activities

The school is committed to impart comprehensive and quality education by means of academic as well as co-curricular activities. At S.B.O.A. along with formal education we spread awareness of the Indian culture by means of celebrating Indian festivals such as Diwali, Dasehara, Navratri. We give equal importance to the celebration to Id, Nagpanchami, Christmas and Holi by performing suitable activities rather than verbal description.

Content Image

Children Day:-

On the occasion of Children’s Day, Child and adolescent health care week was observed in the school. Mrs. Dr. TruptiBorulkar and team visited the school and guided the student on various problems faced by the students due to excessive use of multimedia.

Book Fair at SBOA:-

The Book Fair by the Scholastic publication was organized in the school in the month of November on the occasion of Children’s Day.

Celebration of National Days:-

We at SBOA celebrate national festivals like Independence Day, Republic Day, Marathwada Muktisangram Divas with great pomp and show in order to imbibe the value of patriotism in the students. The students sing patriotic songs and perform various demonstrations. Every year we also celebrate International Yoga Day on 21st June so that the students understand importance of yoga and practice it daily. MarathwadaMuktisangram Divas

Donation of Diwali Sweets:-

Every year we at SBOA appeal the students to bring the sweets to the school so that they can be donated to the children in orphanages and to the old age homes. This drive enables the children to know their social responsibilities.

Exhibitions on various topics at SBOA - s:-

Different Exhibitions are also conducted regularly on various subjects like Science, Geography,Drawing, Mathematics, Sports,Fun with Stories, craft, Work Experiences, Postal Stamps and Food & Nutrition.

First Aid Training for teachers:-

A team of Doctors from International Association of Pediatrician was invited to school to give the lessons to the teachers on First Aid in case of medical emergency.

Dr. HomiBhabha Young Scientist exam for Std VI &Std IX :-

Mumbai Science Teachers’ Association conducts Dr. HomiBhabha Young Scientist Exam for Std VI &StdIX . The aim of the exam is to make the next generation students well versed and aware of the scientific developments globally so that they contribute effectively to the scientific research and environment, enhancement within the communities. FifteenStudents of Std IX and Seven Students of Std VI qualified for Second Level Practical Exam.

National Talent Search Exam :-

One of the Prestigious Exam conducted every year by the Govt. of India. 10 students of our school are successfully cleared National Talent Search Examination first level and they will receive one time scholarship of rupees 1000/-. Master Indrajeet Garse and Master Anshul Kabra has successfully cleared both the Levels and is eligible for the scholarship till his Post Graduation.

Maharashtra Talent Search Exam (MTSE) Std VIII to Std X

Stunning performance of the students at MTSE Examination has added a feather in the school cap.
