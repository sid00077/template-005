About Us

Welcome to the S.B.O.A. PUBLIC SCHOOL AURANGABAD

Thank you for visiting us! Whether you are considering S.B.O.A. as your next school or have already decided to come aboard, we hope you are inspired by our sense of purpose, our commitment to excellence, and our devotion to the development of each student.

Establishment Of The School

The dream of State Bank of India Officers’ Association’s Education Society came true in June-1999 in the form of a promising Educational Institution. Aurangabad School is honored to be the first venture of the SBOA Education Society in Maharashtra. The school was established not only to cater to the needs of the wards of the State Bank of India Officers but also to the society at large. S. B. O. A. Public School, Aurangabad is open to all the children irrespective of their caste, creed, region and religion.

It follows the system of Co-education and runs on the Permanent - Non - Grant basis. With the motto “Educate and Illuminate” the school is providing comprehensive Education to the students for their all-round development since 1999. The School has set high standard for itself to achieve their potential for learning. The school has been channelizing student’s potential and guiding them to attain the ambition they and their parents have by relevant training program, such as practical demonstrations, orientation and competitive activities. A small sapling planted 21 years back has grown into a huge banyan tree. In this short span of time the school has climbed the ladder of success and has reached the summit .170 professionally sound lady teachers are contributing to illuminate the lives of 3650 students ranging between the age group of 3 to 15 years.
